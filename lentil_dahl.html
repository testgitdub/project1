Lentil Dahl

Below are the instructions to prepare this delicious meal.

Ingredients

  • 1 tin of chopped tomatoes
  • 200 g red lentils (uncooked)
  • 300 ml water
  • 1 vegetable stock cube
  • 50 ml low fat coconut milk
  • 2 tblsp ground cumin
  • pinch black pepper
  • fresh corriander for garnish
  • 1 tblsp tumeric

Method

This one pop wonder is quick and easy. Add all content into pot. Let it simmer for 20 minutes until liquids are absorbed by lentil leaving a wholesome and hearty meal. Serve with a portion of basmati rice.
