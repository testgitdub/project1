Spaghetti Bolognese

Below are the instructions to prepare this delicious meal.

Ingredients

  • 200g lean steak mince
  • 1 onion diced
  • 2 red peppers
  • 430g jar Bolognese sauce (no more than 5g sugar and 3g fat â€“ check the label)
  • 100g wholewheat spaghetti
  • sea salt and freshly ground black pepper

Method

Brown the mince and drain off excess oil. Add in onions, peppers and jar of bolognese sauce and fry. Add additional inngredients and simmer for about 20 mins until piping hot. Serve with cooked spaghetti.
